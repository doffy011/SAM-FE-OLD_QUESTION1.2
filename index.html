What is ITPEC Exam?

The Information Technology Professional Examination Council (ITPEC) is an organization that conducts a standardized IT examination across several Asian countries, including Myanmar.

This exam is based on the Japanese Information-Technology Engineers Examination and is internationally recognized, helping engineers validate their skills in Technology, Management, and Strategy.

Whether you are a student or a professional, passing ITPEC exams like IP or FE opens doors to global career opportunities, especially in the Japanese IT market.
